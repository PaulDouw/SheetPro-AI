Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2025

At SheetPro AI, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our Google Sheets Add-on (the "Service").

1. Data Collection
We do not collect or store your spreadsheet data. All your inputs and AI-related activities are processed directly through the AI provider (OpenAI, Gemini, etc.). API keys are stored locally on your device and are never transferred or stored on our servers. Data is transmitted securely via HTTPS.

2. Use of Your Information
Your prompts and API keys are securely sent to your selected AI provider (OpenAI, Gemini, DeepSeek, etc.) for generating responses. We do not log or retain any user data or API keys. Any generated responses from the AI model are displayed in your Google Sheets and are not stored by SheetPro AI.

3. Data Security
All communication between your device, SheetPro AI, and the AI provider is secured with HTTPS (TLS). We do not store any of your data on our servers, and we take all reasonable measures to ensure the security of the information that you send and receive through the Service.

4. Third-Party Services
SheetPro AI integrates with third-party AI providers such as OpenAI, Google Gemini, and DeepSeek. These third-party services may have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them. We are not responsible for the practices or policies of these third-party services.

5. Data Retention
As mentioned, we do not store any data on our servers. The API keys you provide are stored locally in your Google Sheets environment. The data remains within your device, and it is only processed on the fly through your interactions with the Service.

6. Children's Privacy
Our Service is not intended for individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so that we can take appropriate action.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy
SheetPro AI reserves the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be reflected by the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.
